STUDENT BODY CHAPTER SRKR

The ISTE Student Body Chapter at Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju Engineering College (SRKREC) is a vital component of our institution, dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. Affiliated with the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE), our chapter plays a crucial role in enriching the educational experience of engineering students by organizing a diverse range of activities and initiatives. We focus on providing students with opportunities to engage in hands-on learning through workshops, seminars, and conferences that address current industry trends and technological advancements. Our chapter also facilitates networking events with industry professionals, fostering connections that can lead to internships, project collaborations, and career opportunities. Through these efforts, we aim to enhance students' technical skills, promote innovative thinking, and prepare them for the challenges of the modern engineering landscape. Additionally, the ISTE Student Body Chapter at SRKREC serves as a platform for student advocacy, ensuring that their voices are heard and their concerns are addressed, thereby contributing to a more supportive and responsive academic environment. By nurturing a culture of continuous learning and professional growth, we strive to empower our students to excel both academically and in their future careers.
